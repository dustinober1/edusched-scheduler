## üéì Demo Scenario

The portfolio demo showcases a complete university scheduling scenario:

### University Structure
- **3 Departments**: Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics
- **5 Teachers**: Mix of professors and teaching assistants
- **3 Buildings**: Technology, Academic, Science buildings
- **10 Resources**: Classrooms, labs, and lecture halls
- **7 Courses**: Various subjects and difficulty levels
- **265 Students**: Total enrollment across all courses

### Constraints Demonstrated
- **Teacher Preferences**: Days, times, buildings
- **Room Requirements**: Capacity, equipment, amenities
- **Department Rules**: Specific scheduling needs
- **Time Blockers**: Holidays, maintenance, exams

### Features in Action
1. **Data Import**: Upload CSV templates with all university data
2. **Constraint Builder**: Visual rule creation interface
3. **Schedule Generation**: Multiple solver options (heuristic, OR-Tools)
4. **Conflict Resolution**: Real-time detection and manual fixes
5. **Export Functionality**: Complete schedule package download
6. **Real-time Collaboration**: WebSocket-based live updates
